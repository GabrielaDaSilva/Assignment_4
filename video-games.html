My Favorite Games

  1. AFK Arena

    2. AFK Arena is a best in class turn-based-RPG.

    It is similar to Star Wars Galaxy of Heroes and Marvel Strike Force.

    I have been playing this game for over three years and what make it appealing to me is the different characters and their back stories.

    The game is set in the world of Esperia, in which you can acquire various heroes to defeat multiple enemies and clear stages.

    Every hero features unique skills and abilities to help you succeed in battle and you collect resources to upgrade your heros level

    Click here to check out AFK Arena's official Youtube channel featuring animated episodes into the heros lives called Esperia Shorts.

  2. Apex Legends Mobile

    3. Apex Legends Mobile is a strategy battle royale shooter game that features Legendary character-based gameplay, best-in-class squad battles, and fast-paced combat that cemented the game's place as one of the top shooters, all in a mobile package.

    I started playing Apex Mobile since May 2022 so it still very new to me, however, I have managed to quickly clime the ranks to platinum due to being able to play as 3-man squads.

    What I appreaciate about this game is the open world aspect along with the support structure from playing in teams.

    I equally enjoy the strategy aspect where you have to consider the legends abilities in relation to the legend in your squad
